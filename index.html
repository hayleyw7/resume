Versatile software engineer with broad vision and meticulous attention to detail. Always actively learning new technologies and methodologies. Thrives independently in fast-paced environments. Known as "glue" that holds teams together, ensuring every team member's voice is valued and contributes to collective objectives.

Excels at diverse tasks, from planning and agile retrospectives to hands-on development and documentation. Dedicated to crafting efficient, maintainable code that adheres to current best practices, with strong focus on accessibility and inclusivity.

Background

Career-changer with background in marketing and operations in the regulated cannabis industry. Graduated from Turing School of Software & Design, diversity-focused code school. Volunteering

Very active in various STEM networks. Always eager to engage in tech talks, panels, mentoring sessions, coffee chats, and more, continually seeking to contribute to and grow within tech.
